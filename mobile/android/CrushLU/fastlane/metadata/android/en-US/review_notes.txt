Crush.lu is a hybrid Android WebView app for the existing Crush.lu dating platform.

Authentication opens in the user's browser for Google/LuxID/social login and returns to the app with the custom scheme `crushlu://auth`. This avoids OAuth problems inside embedded WebViews and creates the in-app session through a one-time backend code.

Premium subscription CTAs are suppressed in this Android build while `ANDROID_NATIVE_COMMERCE_ENABLED=false`; Play Billing is not enabled in version 1.0.0.

Please use the review account provided in Play Console App access. Keep the account free of real personal messages and real user profile data.
