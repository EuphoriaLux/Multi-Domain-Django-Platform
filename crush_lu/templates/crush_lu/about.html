About Crush.lu

Our Mission

We're bringing back authentic connections in Luxembourg's dating scene.

In a world of endless swiping and superficial matches, Crush.lu takes a different approach. We believe that real relationships start with real conversations, face-to-face.

Why Luxembourg?

Luxembourg is a small, tight-knit community where privacy and discretion matter. Our platform is designed with this in mind, offering a curated experience that respects your privacy while helping you meet compatible people.

The Crush Coach Difference

Every profile on Crush.lu is reviewed by a dedicated Crush Coach. This ensures:

  • Authentic profiles only - no bots, no catfishing
  • Personalized guidance to help you present your best self
  • A safer, more respectful community
  • Support throughout your journey to find connections
